U-Haul Moving Truck Rentals

At Willow Wood Storage Centers, we proudly serve as an authorized U-Haul dealer, providing you with convenient access to U-Haul's comprehensive fleet of rental trucks. Whether you're planning a local move or a long-distance relocation, our partnership with U-Haul ensures you have access to reliable, well-maintained vehicles designed specifically for your moving needs.

Benefits of U-Haul Rental Trucks

- Easier Loading:

U-Haul rental trucks feature innovative designs that make loading 50% easier, with low decks and wide, stable EZ-Load Ramps for effortless access.

- Smooth Ride:

Experience a comfortable journey with U-Haul's Gentle-Ride suspension system and aerodynamic styling that protects your belongings during transport.

- Comfortable Cab:

Enjoy a quiet, spacious cab with independent seating for three passengers, providing comfort for you and your moving crew.

- Convenience and Accessibility:

With over 20,000 locations across the US and Canada, U-Haul offers unparalleled convenience for pickup and return. All trucks run on standard unleaded fuel for easy refueling.

One-Way Truck Rentals

For one-way moves, U-Haul's extensive network of over 20,000 locations across North America makes it simple and cost-effective to drop off your rental at your destination.

The Right Equipment for Your Move

U-Haul works directly with manufacturers to create trucks specifically engineered for household moving. Our vehicles feature lower ground clearance, rounded corners, protective chassis skirts, and comfortable cabs designed for optimal loading efficiency and fuel economy.

Your Safety is Our Priority

Safety is paramount at Willow Wood Storage Centers. All U-Haul trucks 15' and larger, plus our pickup trucks, are equipped with seat belts for three passengers. Our cargo vans and 10' trucks feature comfortable bucket seats with seat belts for two. Patented mirror systems and spacious cabs provide excellent visibility and driving comfort.
